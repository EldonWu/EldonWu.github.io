Finland

Finland is a land of stark contrasts. In the summer months, the sun doesn't set at all in the northernmost parts of the country. In the winter, the opposite happens, and the sun disappears for months. The white snow, bright moon and stars, and - if you're lucky - the colourful Northern Lights, create a surprisingly light and magical nightscape. It has been recognized as the happiest country in the world for several years running.

Finland faces growing crisis with 20,000 stray cats roaming the streets

Finland is grappling with a significant animal welfare issue as an estimated 20,000 stray cats, known as "population cats," wander the streets without a home. These cats are the offspring of unspayed, free-roaming cats, and their uncontrolled breeding has led to large feral colonies across the country.
