Sauna

The word sauna itself is of Finnish origin. It is estimated that there are three million saunas in Finland, for a population of 5.5 million.

You'll be sweating heavily in a sauna, so be sure to drink plenty. Water is the best thing to quench your thirst, but beer, cider and soft drinks are also commonly enjoyed. If you get hungry, cooking sausages either on the open fire or in tin foil directly on the stove is another key part of the sauna experience.
